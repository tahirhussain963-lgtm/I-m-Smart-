Local Shop - Online Store

Project Description

I want to develop a mobile application for my retail shop that allows me to sell at least fifty products online. The application will serve customers within an approximate radius of five kilometers, focusing on local customers and fast delivery. The app should be user-friendly, secure, and optimized for mobile devices. Its main purpose is to increase local sales, manage orders efficiently, and provide a smooth digital shopping experience for customers in the surrounding area.

Customer (User) Features

  • User registration and login (via mobile number or email)
  • Product listing (minimum 50 products)
  • Product details including images, price, and description
  • Category-wise product display
  • Search functionality
  • Add to cart / remove from cart
  • Easy order placement
  • Delivery area restriction (within 5 km radius)
  • Delivery time selection
  • Order history and order tracking
  • Push notifications (order status, offers, updates)
  • Customer support / contact section

Payment Features

  • Cash on Delivery (COD)
  • Online payment integration (EasyPaisa / JazzCash / Bank Transfer)
  • Order confirmation notification after payment

Delivery Features

  • Location-based delivery system
  • 5 km delivery radius control
  • Automatic delivery charge calculation
  • Order status updates (Pending, Confirmed, Out for Delivery, Delivered)

Admin Panel (Shop Owner) Features

  • Secure admin login
  • Add, edit, and delete products
  • Manage product prices and stock
  • View and manage customer orders
  • Update order status
  • Manage customer data
  • Sales reports and order summaries
  • Create and manage discounts and offers
  • Send notifications to users

Technical & General Requirements

  • Secure data handling
  • Fast performance and smooth user experience
  • Clean and simple UI/UX design
  • Android application (with future option to expand to iOS)
  • Scalable backend for future product and area expansion
